Privacy Policy - Quote Ready

Last updated: May 2026

No account required

Quote Ready does not require account creation to use core app features.

Local-first design

Quote Ready is designed to work as a local-first app. Information you enter into Quote Ready, including customer details, quote items, prices, notes, and generated quote content, is stored locally on your device.

How your information is used

Quote Ready does not sell personal information.

Quote Ready does not share personal information with third parties for advertising.

You control when you generate, export, or share PDF quote documents.

Diagnostics and app stability

The app may collect limited diagnostics or crash information to help improve stability and performance.

Your responsibilities

You are responsible for managing your own device security and backups.

Children's privacy

Quote Ready is not directed to children under 13.

Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

support@cleanutilityapps.com
