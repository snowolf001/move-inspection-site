FAQ

Who is this app for?

Rental Inspect is designed for Airbnb hosts and guests, landlords, tenants, and property managers who want a simple way to document rental property conditions with photos, notes, and PDF reports.

Do I need an account?

No. You can use the app without creating an account.

Can I export reports?

Yes. The app lets you generate PDF inspection reports with photos and notes.

Is this legal advice or legal certification?

Rental Inspect helps users document property conditions and organize inspection records. It does not provide legal advice, legal certification, or guarantee acceptance as evidence in any dispute or legal proceeding.
