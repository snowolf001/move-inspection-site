Privacy Policy for Move Inspection

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Move Inspection is a rental property inspection app that helps users document property condition with photos and generate professional move-in and move-out inspection reports. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

Data Stored Locally

Move Inspection does not require an account. All inspection reports, photos, and entered data are stored locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to our servers as part of normal app operation.

You are in full control of your data. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored inspection data from your device.

Camera and Photo Access

Move Inspection requests access to your device camera and photo library solely to allow you to attach photos to inspection reports. Photos are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to any external server.

Analytics and Tracking

Move Inspection and related website pages may use limited analytics tools to understand app and website usage, improve performance, and enhance the user experience.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Any information that may be collected is not shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Service providers used for analytics or technical operations may process limited usage data as described above.
