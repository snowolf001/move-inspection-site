Privacy Policy for DinnerReady

Last updated: May 2026

DinnerReady is a meal planning application developed by Clean Utility Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use the service.

Information We Collect

DinnerReady does not require account creation.

The app may process the following information to generate meal plans:

  • Meal preferences
  • Dietary preferences
  • Cuisine selections
  • Ingredient preferences and restrictions
  • Meal planning settings

How Information Is Used

Information submitted through the app is used only for generating meal plans, grocery lists, and related meal recommendations.

Some requests may be processed through third-party AI services in order to generate meal planning results.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

We do not use your information for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Some meal planning preferences may be temporarily processed on backend servers in order to provide app functionality.

Children's Privacy

DinnerReady is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

cleanutilityapps@gmail.com
