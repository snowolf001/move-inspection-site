Data Deletion Request

If you would like to request deletion of your data, please contact us at:

cleanutilityapps@gmail.com

Please include the following information:

  • Your recovery code (recommended)
  • Your name used in the app (optional)

The recovery code is the most reliable way for us to identify your data.

If you do not have your recovery code, please provide as much detail as possible and we will attempt to locate your data.

We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing the request.

We will process your request within 7 days. All associated data will be permanently deleted.
