Account deletion

Delete Your Account

Passeo accounts are tied to a local identity stored on your device. The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the app. If you no longer have access to the app or your local identity, you can submit a request and we will handle it manually.

Preferred — fastest

If you still have app access

Deleting your account from inside the app is immediate and does not require any manual review. Use this path whenever possible.

1

Open Passeo on your device.

2

Go to your Profile (tap your name or avatar in the bottom navigation).

3

Scroll down and tap Delete My Account.

4

Read the confirmation and confirm the deletion. This action cannot be undone.

Fallback — no app access

If you no longer have app access

If you have lost access to the app or your local identity and cannot use the in-app deletion flow, you can request account deletion by emailing us. To help us locate your account, please include as many of the following details as you can.

Club name — the name of the club you were a member of in Passeo.

Member display name — the name shown for your account in the app.

Approximate join date — roughly when you joined the club (month and year is fine).

Recovery code — if you still have it, this is the most reliable way for us to locate your account.

A recovery code is helpful but not required. If you do not have one, provide as much of the other information as you can and we will do our best to locate your account.

Email us to request deletion

We will process your request within 7 days of receiving sufficient identifying information.

What happens to your data

When your account is deleted, your personal data is removed or permanently anonymized. Here is what to expect.

🗑

Personal information is deleted. Your display name, recovery code, profile data, and any other information that identifies you personally will be permanently removed.

📊

Historical activity may be anonymized. Attendance records and participation history that contribute to club reports may be retained in anonymized form only — stripped of any personally identifying information — so that aggregate club statistics remain accurate.
