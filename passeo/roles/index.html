Roles and permissions

Passeo is designed for clubs and recurring groups. Clear roles help everyone understand who manages the club, who helps operate it, and what regular members can do inside the app.

At a glance

  • Owner controls club setup and high-level management.
  • Admin helps run the club day to day.
  • Member joins, checks in, and participates.

Role overview

These are the three main roles in Passeo. Depending on how your club is configured, owners and admins may handle operations together while members focus on joining sessions and tracking participation.

Owner

Club leadership

The owner is the main club manager and typically created the club or is responsible for overall control.

  • Manage club settings
  • Create and manage sessions
  • Manage members and roles
  • Review activity and reports
Admin

Operational support

Admins help run the club. They usually handle member management, check-in support, and session operations.

  • Help manage members
  • Help operate sessions
  • Support attendance workflows
  • Reduce owner workload
Member

Everyday participation

Members join the club, participate in sessions, and interact with the experience that matters most on a daily basis.

  • Join with a code
  • View available sessions
  • Check in when eligible
  • Track participation history

Practical guidance

Best practice: keep the number of admins limited and assign the role only to trusted organizers. Members should have a simple, low-friction experience: join the club, see sessions, and check in without confusion.

If your club has only one organizer, the owner role may cover almost everything. If your club is larger, admins can help with day-to-day operations while the owner keeps overall control.
