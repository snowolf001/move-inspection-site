Passeo guide

Frequently asked questions

A quick guide to the most common questions about Passeo, including clubs, roles, sessions, attendance, participation, and member access.

General

What is Passeo?

Passeo is a club management app designed to make it easier to organize members, sessions, attendance, and participation in one place.

Who is Passeo for?

Passeo is designed for clubs and groups that need a simple way to manage activities, member participation, and session records without adding unnecessary complexity.

Does Passeo only work for one type of club?

No. Passeo is flexible enough for many kinds of clubs and recurring groups, including activity clubs, community groups, classes, volunteer teams, and small organizations.

Roles & permissions

What roles exist in Passeo?

Clubs in Passeo can use role-based access so different people can have different levels of responsibility. Common roles may include owner, host, host, and member.

See Roles for a more complete overview.

Can every member manage sessions?

Not usually. Session management is typically limited to higher-permission roles such as host, host, or owner, depending on how the club is configured.

Can permissions differ from club to club?

Yes. A club may choose how responsibilities are handled internally, so the exact workflow can vary depending on the organization's needs.

Sessions & attendance

What is the difference between joining and checking in?

Joining means a member plans to attend a session. Checking in means the member actually attended and the attendance was recorded.

Can a session have a maximum number of attendees?

Yes. Clubs can use capacity limits for sessions. Once the session reaches its limit, further joins can be locked to prevent overbooking.

Does Passeo keep past session records?

Yes. Past sessions can be retained so clubs can review attendance history, participation totals, and activity records over time.

Can attendance and participation be tracked separately?

Yes. Depending on the club's setup, Passeo can distinguish between simple check-ins and broader participation tracking.

Members & access

Can members see upcoming sessions?

Yes. Members can view upcoming sessions and review details such as timing, location, and whether the session is still available.

Can organizers review member history?

Depending on role permissions, organizers may be able to review participation history, attendance records, and other club-related member activity.

Is Passeo meant to reduce manual tracking?

Yes. One of Passeo's main goals is to reduce manual coordination and make attendance, sessions, and role-based responsibilities easier to manage in a consistent way.
