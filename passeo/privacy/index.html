Privacy Policy for Passeo

Passeo may collect limited information needed to provide app functionality and improve the product experience.

This may include:

How We Use Information

Information is used to support core app features such as check-ins, attendance tracking, reporting, and general product improvement.

Analytics and Tracking

Passeo and related website pages may use limited analytics or tracking tools to understand app and website usage, improve performance, and enhance the user experience.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be processed by service providers that support app functionality, hosting, analytics, or related technical operations.

Data Storage

Some data may be stored locally on your device, and some data may be processed by backend services required for app functionality.
