Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Passeo is a club session and attendance management app designed to help organize sessions, track participation, and manage club workflows.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the app:

  • Membership and session data
  • Attendance records
  • Basic usage analytics

How We Use Information

Data is used to power features like sessions, check-ins, and reporting.

Analytics

Limited analytics are used to improve product performance and usability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers.
